Dedication to Dignity and Respect

Respect and dignity form the cornerstone of Everest Homecare's approach to caregiving.

Empowering Independence with Exceptional Care

Everest Home care. LLC respect the rights of individuals to live their life to the fullest even they already have an limitations. All employees from Everest Home Care are individually insured and bonded. Our commitment to excellence in home care makes an undeniable difference to the clients we are privileged to serve.

Objectives

Enhance quality of life, promote independence, ensure safety, and continually improve care programs.

Beneficiaries

Individuals with limitations, including the elderly, those recovering from surgery or illness, and those with chronic health conditions, as well as their families and caregivers.

Strategy

Provide personalized care plans, employ skilled and compassionate caregivers, offer continuous training, conduct regular assessments, maintain open communication, and engage with local communities for holistic support.

Mission , Vision & Values

Live In Care

Everest HomeCare understands how important and comforting it is having the same caregiver each and everyday. Building a relationship, understanding a client's likes and dislikes and developing a routine are extremely important junctions of quality home Care. We provide the uttermost continuity of care with our live-in service. Many accident happen when no one else is present or in the middle of the night. We provide peace of mind for you at anytime and best possible care all day and night long.
Everest HC is a full service agency. We will arrange for the caregiver's time off, provide transportation and arrange for a quality replacement leave everything to us for seamless, uninterrupted live-in care.

Our Caregivers

We provide certified Caregivers that are able to meet your loved one's personal care and homemaker needs. Our Caregiver provides personal assistance and care such as assistance with bathing and dressing.
Our Caregiver goes through criminal background checks including but not limited to fingerprinting, and must be a U.S citizen or authorized to work in the United States.
