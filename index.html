Mission and Vision

Mission

  • Dedicated Caregiving: We are committed to providing dedicated, compassionate caregiving services to ensure the well-being and comfort of our clients.
  • Continuity of Care: We understand the importance of consistency in care. Our mission is to provide the same caregiver each day, building trust and a strong client-caregiver relationship.
  • Safety and Peace of Mind: Everest Home Care aims to offer peace of mind to families by providing care around the clock. We prioritize safety, especially during critical times, such as late nights.
  • Comprehensive Support: As a full-service agency, we go above and beyond. We arrange for caregivers' time off, handle transportation, and ensure seamless, uninterrupted live-in care.
  • Empowering Independence: Our mission is to empower clients to maintain their independence while receiving the support and care they need, allowing them to live life to the fullest.

Vision

  • Leading Industry Innovator: Everest Home Care aspires to lead the industry through innovative approaches to caregiving, setting new standards for quality and excellence.
  • Positive Impact: We envision making a positive impact not only in the lives of our clients but also in the broader community. We strive to be a force for good in the world.
  • Elevating Care Standards: Our vision involves continuously raising the bar for care standards, ensuring that every client receives the highest level of service and attention.
  • Empowered Caregivers: We aim to create an environment where our caregivers feel empowered, valued, and equipped to provide exceptional care, ultimately benefiting our clients.
  • Community of Trust: Everest Home Care envisions building a strong, trustworthy community where families and clients feel confident in our ability to deliver top-tier caregiving services.

Live In Care

Everest HomeCare understands how important and comforting it is having the same caregiver each and everyday. Building a relationship, understanding a client's likes and dislikes and developing a routine are extremely important junctions of quality home Care. We provide the uttermost continuity of care with our live-in service. Many accident happen when no one else is present or in the middle of the night. We provide peace of mind for you at anytime and best possible care all day and night long.
Everest HC is a full service agency. We will arrange for the caregiver's time off, provide transportation and arrange for a quality replacement leave everything to us for seamless, uninterrupted live-in care.

Our Caregivers

We provide certified Caregivers that are able to meet your loved one's personal care and homemaker needs. Our Caregiver provides personal assistance and care such as assistance with bathing and dressing.
Our Caregiver goes through criminal background checks including but not limited to fingerprinting, and must be a U.S citizen or authorized to work in the United States.
